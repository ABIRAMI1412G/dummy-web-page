TOKYO REVENGERS

"Tokyo Revengers" is an anime series based on the manga by Ken Wakui. It follows the story of Takemichi Hanagaki, who discovers he has the ability to travel back in time to his middle school days. He tries to change the future by preventing tragic events involving his friends and the girl he loves. The series combines elements of delinquency, time travel, and drama, making it popular among fans of action-packed storytelling with emotional depth.

DEMON SLAYER

"Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba," created by Koyoharu Gotouge, is a Japanese manga and anime series that follows Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy who becomes a demon slayer after demons slaughter his family and turn his sister, Nezuko, into a demon. The series explores themes of family, perseverance, and the struggle between good and evil, with Tanjiro seeking a cure for Nezuko and vengeance against the demons. Acclaimed for its compelling story and high-quality animation, the manga ran from 2016 to 2020, while the anime adaptation, produced by Ufotable, has gained a global following.
